About Me

Click to see a photo of me!

Hello! I'm Riccardo Bean, a Computer Science student at NYU Tandon School of Engineering, pursuing my Bachelor's degree with minors in Astronomy, Mathematics, and Game Engineering.

I was born and raised on the colorful island of Burano, a small yet vibrant part of Venice, Italy. At sixteen, I moved to New York for what was supposed to be a single semester abroad: a short adventure that unexpectedly became a turning point in my life.

Adapting to a new city, culture, and language was challenging at first, but it also taught me resilience, curiosity, and independence. What started as a temporary stay evolved into a long-term journey of growth, leading me to graduate high school in the U.S. and continue my studies at NYU.

Today, my interests lie at the intersection of technology and creativity. I love exploring how systems work, whether that's through game design, software development, cybersecurity, or astronomy. I enjoy creating projects that blend logic, design, and storytelling into meaningful digital experiences.
