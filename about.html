About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Riccardo and I am a freshman at NYU Tandon studying Computer Science.

I was born and raised in a small island called Burano, in Venice, Italy.

However, I moved to New York at 16 for what should have been only a semester abroad, but turned out to be a completely life-changing decision.

After an initial challenging time, I decided to extend my stay until high school graduation and beyond.

Now, I am pursuing my Bachelor's Degree at New York University with a major in Computer Science and minors in Astronomy and Cybersecurity.
